Your music library isn't just background noise. In Skate Beats, it's the level designer.

## No Two Songs Are The Same

Here's the wild thing about Skate Beats – you don't just play to the music. The game **listens** to your music and builds the level around it.

Throw on some lo-fi hip hop? You get chill, flowing lines with gentle gaps. Crank up death metal? Congratulations, you now have vertical insanity.

The bass drops, the city drops.

## How We Actually Do It (Without Magic)

Okay, there might be some magic involved.

But mostly, it's a lot of audio analysis:

### Beat Detection
We analyze your music in real-time, detecting BPM, key changes, and those sweet sweet bass drops. This determines the *pace* of the level.

### Frequency Analysis
High frequencies? Rail placements. Low frequencies? Ramps and gaps. Mid-tones? That's where the collectibles live.

### Dynamic Difficulty
If you're crushing it, the game gets harder. Miss too many combos? The level chills out. It's like a rubber band that stretches and contracts based on your skill.

## The City Moves to Your Rhythm

You're not just skating – you're **dancing** with the environment.
